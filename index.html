about sena

Know More Sena Cafe

We started operation in 2019. The name of sena is taken from the name of the owner, a mime artist and old actor, Sena A Utoyo. The concept used is to create an atmosphere like home so that customers can be comfortable and safe. Our vision and mission is to spread our passion for high quality coffee and provide customers with a wide variety of home-cooked meals. Sena cafe is very suitable for workers and students to study because of the calm atmosphere.
